Oliver Haimson

Oliver Haimson conducts social computing research focused on social media, online identity, and gender in the context of life transitions. He is a recipient of a National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship, a Rob Kling Memorial Fellowship, a Eugene Cota-Robles Fellowship, and a Faculty Mentor Program Fellowship. He works with Dr. Gillian Hayes in the STAR Group (Social and Technological Action Research).
